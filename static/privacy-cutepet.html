This Privacy Policy describes how 萌宠相机 (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application on Android and iOS. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Overview

萌宠相机 is a pet photo camera application. Core features include in-app photo capture, local and cloud photo management, pet sound effects, AI-assisted pet copywriting, and a photo palette that displays capture metadata (time, device, and location when available).

Important: The App supports photo capture only. We do not provide in-app video recording. On some devices, the system camera permission dialog may mention “record video” because of platform wording; that does not mean the App records video.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect or process the following information:

  • Account and device identifiers: an anonymous device UUID generated on first launch, session token, and user ID returned by our login API.
  • Photos you capture: image files, thumbnails, and related metadata (capture time, device model, and GPS coordinates when location is available).
  • Custom audio (optional): short recordings you create in the Sound Library for custom pet sound effects.
  • App settings: preferences such as sound volume, shutter sound, burst mode, and timer settings stored locally on your device.
  • Technical and usage data: information needed to operate API requests (e.g., app version, request timestamps, encrypted API payloads).

3. Device Permissions and Purposes

We request access only when needed for the features below:

Permission Platform Purpose Required?
Camera Android / iOS Capture pet photos using the in-app camera preview and shutter. Yes, for taking photos
Photos / Media Library Android / iOS Save captures to your gallery, browse photos taken with the App, and sync local albums. Yes, for gallery features
Location (When In Use) Android / iOS Obtain coarse or fine location to embed GPS data in photo EXIF and show capture location in the photo palette. Optional
Media Location Android Read location metadata from photos in the gallery when EXIF or media location is available. Optional
Microphone Android / iOS Record short custom pet sound effects in the Sound Library. Not used during normal photo capture. Optional
Storage (legacy) Android 12 and below Read and write photos on older Android versions where scoped storage rules differ. Conditional
Internet / Network Android / iOS Sign in, upload photos or audio, sync cloud records, fetch sound categories, and use AI text generation. Yes, for online features
You may deny optional permissions (such as Location or Microphone) and still use photo capture. Some features—location on the palette, custom sound recording, or cloud sync—may be limited if related permissions or network access are unavailable.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide camera capture, preview, editing, and gallery management.
  • Save photos locally and, when enabled, upload them to our servers for cloud backup and cross-device access.
  • Generate AI-assisted pet captions and palette presentation based on images you submit.
  • Store and play pet sound effects, including sounds you record or select.
  • Attach device and location metadata to photos for display in the palette export card.
  • Maintain your session, improve stability, and prevent abuse of our services.
  • Respond to support requests when you contact customer service.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • On your device: photos, settings, UUID, and session data may be stored in app storage and SharedPreferences (or equivalent).
  • On our servers: uploaded photos, cloud gallery records, and related URLs may be retained while your account is active or as required to provide the service.
  • Retention: we keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required by law or legitimate business needs (e.g., dispute resolution).

You may delete photos from the in-app gallery and request removal of associated cloud records where the App provides delete functionality.

6. Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these cases:

  • Service infrastructure: our backend APIs and file hosting/CDN used to upload, store, and deliver images and audio.
  • Platform services: geocoding or device information providers used to convert GPS coordinates into readable place names or to write device metadata.
  • Sharing you initiate: when you use system share features to send images to other apps.
  • Legal requirements: when required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

7. Security
